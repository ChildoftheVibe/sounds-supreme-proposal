Technology Stack

Enterprise-Grade Tools.
Startup-Friendly Costs.

Every tool in this stack was chosen for reliability, scalability, and industry-standard security. These are the same tools used by Spotify, Netflix, and Apple.

FB
Backend

Firebase (Google)

Authentication, real-time database, cloud functions, and user management. Industry standard. Scales to millions automatically.

FL
Frontend & Mobile

Flutter

One codebase, two apps. iOS and Android built simultaneously — meaning half the development time and cost compared to building separately.

GC
Media Hosting

Google Cloud Storage

All audio files live here. Encrypted at rest, access-controlled, and integrated with BunnyCDN for fast global delivery.

🐰
Content Delivery (CDN)

BunnyCDN

Delivers music to listeners with low latency no matter where they are. Think of it as a turbo boost for audio streaming.

$

Stripe + Apple IAP

Stripe handles web/Android payments. Apple's In-App Purchase handles iOS — required by App Store policy. Both are PCI-compliant by default.

🔍
Search

Algolia

Powers the music search and genre discovery engine. Returns results in milliseconds — even with thousands of tracks in the library.
